Fried Egg Recipe

An easy and quick method to make the perfect fried egg, great for any meal.

Preparation Ingredients Instructions Nutrition

Preparation Time

  • Cooking: 3 minutes
  • Total: Approximately 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • large egg
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil. Let it melt and spread evenly.
  2. Crack the egg: Carefully crack the egg into a small bowl to avoid shell pieces, then gently pour it into the pan.
  3. Cook the egg: Allow the egg white to set while keeping the yolk soft. For a sunny-side-up egg, do not flip. For over-easy, flip gently after 2 minutes and cook for an additional 30 seconds.
  4. Season: Sprinkle salt and pepper over the egg as it cooks.
  5. Serve: Remove the egg from the pan using a spatula and place it on a plate. Serve immediately.
